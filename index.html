The Path

Your mind is not broken. It is doing what it learned to do under pressure. The goal is not to erase your past, it is to stop your body from reacting as if the past is happening now.

Reset the loop

We target the replay cycle: the moment, the body surge, the rumination, the crash. You learn how to interrupt it without white-knuckling.

Repair attachment pain

Loss does not only hurt, it bonds. We work with the subconscious attachment system so separation stops feeling like danger.

Rebuild a future self

Trauma shrinks your timeline. We expand it again, so you can plan, desire, sleep, and function without constant internal negotiation.

What to expect

A timeline for the six-week program. Grief is not linear, but structure helps your nervous system trust the process.

  1. Week 1: Safety and Stabilization Sleep support, calming the body response, defining your goals, and building your first “interrupt” tools.
  2. Week 2: The Shock Layer Working with disbelief, mental replay, “how did this happen?” loops, and the body’s startle response.
  3. Week 3: Anger and Boundary Repair Anger as a signal, not a personality. We rebuild boundaries and reduce self-blame spirals.
  4. Week 4: Bargaining and Rumination The “if I had” loop, obsessive analysis, and attachment panic. We quiet the negotiation engine.
  5. Week 5: Sadness and Meaning Letting grief move without drowning you. We build tolerance for emotion without collapse.
  6. Week 6: Integration and Forward Identity A future-focused self-state, relapse prevention, and a plan for triggers, anniversaries, and contact events.
