The Path

Trauma is not only an event, it is a pattern in the nervous system. The goal is not to erase memory, it is to stop your body from responding as if the event is happening now.

Interrupt the loop

We work with the sequence, trigger, body surge, replay, rumination, crash. You learn interruption tools that do not require willpower battles.

Repair attachment pain

Loss can create a biological alarm state. We calm that system, so separation stops feeling like danger, and sleep does not depend on exhaustion.

Rebuild future identity

Trauma collapses the future into “getting through today.” We rebuild your internal stability so you can plan, decide, and move without constant internal negotiation.

What to expect

A structured six-week program for grief, intrusive looping, insomnia, and attachment pain. Many clients notice the themes resemble stages of grief. We use that structure while respecting that grief is not linear.

  1. Week 1: Stabilization and Sleep Support A calm baseline, tools for overwhelm, and a plan for nighttime looping and intrusive replay.
  2. Week 2: The Shock Layer Reducing the “this cannot be real” loop, body startle responses, and mental replay patterns.
  3. Week 3: Anger and Boundary Repair Anger as information. We reduce self-blame spirals and rebuild boundaries without hardening.
  4. Week 4: Bargaining and Rumination Quieting the negotiation engine: “if I had,” “what if,” checking, scanning, and attachment panic.
  5. Week 5: Grief Tolerance and Meaning Letting grief move through without collapse, numbness, or getting stuck inside the feeling.
  6. Week 6: Integration and Forward Identity Relapse prevention, trigger planning, and a future-focused self-state you can return to on demand.
