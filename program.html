The 6-Week Trauma Recovery Program

Designed for grief after relationship rupture, divorce, or death, plus intrusive looping, insomnia, reliving the moment, and attachment pain. Structured, calm, and focused on measurable relief.

What this program is

A six-week framework that begins with stabilization, then reduces replay and attachment alarm, then builds forward identity. Grief is not linear, but structure helps the nervous system trust progress.

Who it fits

People who feel stuck in mental replay, body-based alarm, sleep disruption, rumination, or attachment panic after a loss or rupture.

What it is not

Not a quick “positive mindset” fix. Not spiritual language. Not past life regression. This is grounded, private work.

  1. Week 1: Stabilization and Sleep Support Calm baseline, first interruption tools, sleep plan for nighttime looping and intrusive replay.
  2. Week 2: The Shock Layer Reducing disbelief loops, startle response, and “reliving the moment” activation.
  3. Week 3: Anger and Boundary Repair Anger as information. Lowering self-blame, rebuilding boundaries without hardening.
  4. Week 4: Bargaining and Rumination Quieting the negotiation engine: “if I had,” “what if,” checking, scanning, and attachment panic.
  5. Week 5: Grief Tolerance and Meaning Letting grief move through without collapse, numbness, or getting stuck inside the feeling.
  6. Week 6: Integration and Forward Identity Relapse prevention, trigger planning, and a future-focused self-state you can return to.
