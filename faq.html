FAQ

Clear answers, no hype.

1) What is hypnotherapy?

A guided method for working with attention, relaxation, and subconscious learning so the nervous system can adopt new responses.

2) Will I be unconscious or out of control?

No. Most people feel focused and calm. You can speak, move, or stop at any time.

3) What does hypnosis feel like?

Often like deep relaxation with heightened focus, similar to being absorbed in a book or drifting before sleep.

4) Does it work if I overthink?

Yes. We work with the overthinking pattern directly, and we use structure so you do not have to “try” to relax.

5) How is this different from talk therapy?

Talk therapy can be excellent. Hypnotherapy adds subconscious learning and state-based work to reduce automatic reactions.

6) How many sessions do I need?

It depends on goals and intensity. Many clients choose the six-week structure for stability and momentum.

7) Can hypnotherapy help with insomnia?

Often, yes, especially when insomnia is driven by hypervigilance, rumination, or nervous system activation.

8) What if I cry or get emotional?

That can be normal. We work with pacing and regulation so emotion can move without overwhelm.

9) Do you offer virtual sessions?

Yes. Virtual sessions are private and effective when the environment is quiet and uninterrupted.

10) What happens in a free consult?

We confirm fit, clarify goals, and map what week one would look like if we work together.
